Menstruation Education

Making menstrual health inclusive for all genders.

TIMELINE

6 weeks, January-February 2023

TOOLS

Adobe Illustrator, Figma

SKILLS

User Research, UX Design, Wireframing, Prototyping, Ideation & Synthesis

The Problem

The challenge was to create the front page of an informational website about menstruation that looks simultaneously professional and friendly and would appeal to all adolescent individuals who are starting to or are about to experience menstruation, including those who identify as non-binary or transgender. I wanted to encourage users within that target audience to learn about menstruation and reduce the shame associated with it, learn about how to manage their symptoms, and feel less alone in their experience.

The Solution

Menstruation Education is a gender-inclusive educational website that provides information about what to expect for an individual's first menstruation, in addition to resources about how to manage symptoms. Additionally there are anecdotes such as first period stories, a guide to navigating the period aisle, a tool that helps locate free menstrual product dispensers, and more.
